An adorable fox: Verin

Info

Verin is a half fox half human hybrid, being 15 (as of my current age), about ~170 cms (or ~5'6) in height and approximately ~54kgs (or ~119lbs) in weight.

Physique

  • Hair
    • Verin's hair reaches to the front of their eyes at the front, stopping at the ear at the sides while laying quite flat at the back, where hair comes out from about the end of their neck.
    • Verin's hair is brown and has pudding-like texture, being soft to the touch.
  • Body and muscles
    • Verin is quite slim, making it easier for him to go through small gaps.
    • They only have muscles on their upper arms and thighs.

Personality

Verin is a nice, but shy male fox hybrid, trying to be in favor of others. They tend to make bad moves from time to time, but are extremely loyal and wouldn't dare backstab their friends.

They like to help out others by any means, be it physical or mental.

Features

Verin (as a hybrid of fox and human) has 2 ears - listening carefully for any sound - and a tail - swishing around as an emotion-indicator.
